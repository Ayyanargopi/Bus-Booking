Kerala Most visited places

Munnar ,Kerala

Munnar is one of the most beautiful hill stations in Kerala. Snuggled at 6000 feet, the town has an easy charm. The gradient and the salubrious weather of this hill are perfect for tea farming.

Kochi ,Kerala

One of the top tourist places in Kerala, Fort Kochi, is often the gateway to God’s Own Country. The Queen of the Arabian has multiple personalities. The city has a history, heritage, and multi-ethnic culture

Wayanad ,Kerala

Wayanad is one of the reasons why Kerala is God’s Own Country. The ‘land of paddy fields in Malayalam is one of the greenest places to visit in Kerala. The scenic beauty and thrilling adventures make it a favorite among visitors.

Kovalam ,Kerala

Listed among the best destinations in Kerala, Kovalam is a beauty and one offering cherished experiences. The place offers a lot to tourists. Also, go swimming or bask in the warm sunshine.

Alleppey ,Kerala

Alleppey tops the list of best tourist attractions in Kerala. The city was titled the ‘Venice of the East. The backwaters in the area are perfect for a houseboat vacation.

Thiruvananthapuram ,Kerala

A city thriving with energy yet co-existing with ancient history, Thiruvananthapuram is among the best tourist places in Kerala. The capital city is one of India’s most beautiful and greenest cities
